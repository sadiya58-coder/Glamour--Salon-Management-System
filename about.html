About Glamour

Who We Are

Born from a Passion for Beauty

Glamour was founded in 2012 with a single vision — to create a space where every person who walks through our doors leaves feeling more confident, radiant, and celebrated. What began as a boutique studio in New Delhi has grown into a full-service luxury salon trusted by thousands of clients across India.

Our philosophy is simple: beauty is not a standard to meet — it is a story to tell. Our team of master artists, stylists, and therapists are trained to listen, understand your individuality, and craft experiences tailored uniquely to you.
